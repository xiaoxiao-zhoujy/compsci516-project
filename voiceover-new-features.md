# Demo Script – New Features

## Reading Stats
"On the profile page, we now show reading stats grouped into three categories — My Shelf, which tracks how many books you've read, are currently reading, or want to read; Activity, showing your reviews posted and average rating; and Insights, which surfaces your favorite genre and how many books you've finished this year."

## Annual Reading Goal
"Below that, you can set an annual reading goal. I'll enter 24 books for 2026. Once saved, a progress bar appears showing how many you've finished toward your target."

## Reading Progress Tracker
"For books you're currently reading, you can now log your current page and start date directly from your profile. As you update your page, the progress bar fills in."

## Start / Finish Date Tracking
"On the book detail page, when you mark a book as currently reading, today's date is automatically recorded as your start date. When you finish and mark it as read, the finish date is saved — and that's what feeds the 'Finished This Year' stat."

## Edit / Delete Reviews
"You can now edit or delete your own reviews. Clicking Edit opens a modal where you can update the text, rating, and spoiler flag. Delete removes it with a confirmation prompt."

## Spoiler Toggle
"When writing a review, check 'Contains spoilers' to flag it. On the book page, spoiler reviews are hidden behind a Show Spoiler button so other readers aren't caught off guard."
